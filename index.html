Common Questions

Got Questions?

We've answered the most common queries below. Still have doubts? Feel free to call us or walk into either of our shops — we're always happy to help.

📞 Talk to Us
How long does dry cleaning take? +
Standard dry cleaning takes 48–72 hours depending on the garment type. For express service, most items are ready within 24 hours for an additional charge of 50%.
Do you offer home pickup and delivery? +
Yes! We offer free home pickup and delivery within a 5 km radius of our shops. For areas beyond that, a small delivery charge applies. You can schedule via call or WhatsApp.
Are my clothes insured while in your care? +
Absolutely. All garments left with us are fully insured against damage or loss during the cleaning process. We take full responsibility from the moment we receive your clothes.
What if my garment is damaged during cleaning? +
In the rare event of damage caused by our process, we will compensate you based on the garment's current value. Our team is trained to prevent such situations, and we handle every item with utmost care.
Do you use eco-friendly solvents? +
Yes, we use environment-friendly, non-toxic cleaning agents that are safe for fabrics, people, and the planet. We are committed to sustainable practices in all our operations.
Can I drop off clothes anytime during shop hours? +
Yes, walk-ins are always welcome! Both our shops are open Monday–Friday 8AM–9PM, Saturday 9AM–8PM, and Sunday 10AM–6PM. No appointment needed for drop-offs.
Do you offer subscription or monthly plans? +
Yes! We have monthly subscription plans for regular customers — great for families and professionals. Plans start at ₹999/month and include weekly pickups, priority processing, and a 20% discount on all services.

Meet the Owner
Rajesh Kumar
Sharma
Founder & Master Cleaner · AquaPress

With over 15 years in the garment care industry, Rajesh started AquaPress with a simple vision — to provide the residents of Lucknow with a laundry service they could genuinely trust. What began as a small shop in Hazratganj has grown into one of the city's most beloved dry cleaning establishments.

Trained in advanced garment care techniques in Delhi and certified by the Textile Care Allied Trades Association (TCATA), Rajesh personally oversees quality control at both shop locations. He believes that clean clothes aren't just about appearance — they're about confidence and dignity.
