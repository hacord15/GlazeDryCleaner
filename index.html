Common Questions

Got Questions?

We've answered the most common queries below. Still unsure? Walk into any of our shops or message us on WhatsApp — we're always happy to help.

Chat on WhatsApp
How long does dry cleaning take? +
Standard dry cleaning takes 48–72 hours. Express service is ready in 24 hours for +50% charge.
Do you offer home pickup and delivery? +
Yes! Free home pickup within 5 km of our shops. Book via call or WhatsApp at +91 98765 43210.
Are my clothes insured while in your care? +
All garments are fully insured against damage or loss during the cleaning process.
What if my garment is damaged? +
In the rare event of damage, we compensate based on the garment's current value. Our trained team prevents such situations.
Do you use eco-friendly solvents? +
Yes — we use environment-friendly, non-toxic cleaning agents safe for fabrics, people, and the planet.
Do you offer monthly subscription plans? +
Yes! Plans from ₹999/month — weekly pickups, priority processing, and 20% discount on all services.
How can I inquire about franchise opportunities? +
Visit our Franchise section below or call us directly at +91 98765 43210. We'd love to discuss partnership opportunities with you.
